Watchlist functionality:
    #add         : Add items to the watchlist
    #remove      : Remove items from the watchlist
    #done        : Mark items on the watchlist as done

    usage: watchlist add MOVIE_NAME_OR_ID_OR_KEYWORD [-keyword] [-Deadline] [-Event] [-start <Date1>] [-end <Date2>]
           watchlist remove MOVIE_NAME_KEYWORD [-keyword]
           watchlist done [-Deadline <index of movie in the watchlist>]
